Agust Siregar

I'm a PhD candidate in Chemical Biology at Velema Lab, Radboud University, the Netherlands. My PhD is funded by the TargetRNA Marie Skłodowska-Curie Action (MSCA) network, where I focus on method development for transcriptome-wide RNA–small molecule interaction profiling - with applications in antibiotic/drug discovery that target RNA.

My work sits at the interface of RNA biology, chemical biology, and bioinformatics. Mostly work with SHAPE-MaPseq and DMS-MaPseq — a chemical probing approach that reads RNA secondary structure at single-nucleotide resolution across the transcriptome — to study how small molecules selectively engage RNA targets in living cells.
